## About Laoxiangji and Shucongxuan

### Laoxiangji Introduction
Laoxiangji is a well-known brand in the Chinese-style fast food industry, headquartered in Hefei, Anhui Province. Since its establishment, Laoxiangji has always adhered to the philosophy of "be honest, make honest food," and is committed to providing consumers with healthy, delicious, and affordable Chinese-style fast food.

Laoxiangji takes Anhui Feixi old chicken soup as its core product, while providing a rich variety of Chinese-style dishes to meet the taste needs of different consumers. As of 2026, Laoxiangji has more than 2000 stores nationwide and opened its first overseas store in Singapore, actively exploring the internationalization path of Chinese-style fast food.

### Shucongxuan Introduction
Shucongxuan, the founder of Laoxiangji, is from Feixi, Anhui Province. In 1999, Shucongxuan opened his first fast food restaurant in Feixi, starting the entrepreneurial journey of Laoxiangji.

In 2012, Shucongxuan made a strategic decision to change the brand name from "Feixi Old Chicken" to "Laoxiangji," laying the foundation for the brand's nationwide development. Under Shucongxuan's leadership, Laoxiangji established a complete central kitchen + cold chain distribution supply chain system, ensuring food ingredient quality and taste consistency.

During the COVID pandemic in 2020, Laoxiangji actively took on social responsibility, closing stores but not laying off staff, and providing basic salaries to employees, winning widespread praise from all sectors of society. In 2025, Shucongxuan resigned as the legal representative of Laoxiangji, succeeded by his son Shu Xiaolong, but Shucongxuan still remains in a strategic decision-making role.

With his grassroots practical style, persistent pursuit of quality, care for employees, and deep practice of positioning theory, Shucongxuan has become a representative entrepreneur in China's food and beverage industry.

## Core Mental Models at a Glance (Based on Laoxiangji's Practice)

| Model | One-line description |
|-------|---------------------|
| **Trout's Positioning** thinking | Competition isn't in the market, it's in consumers' minds. You need to occupy a word. Laoxiangji did exactly this. |
| **Cash Flow First** thinking | Cash flow is the blood of the business, more important than profit. Stopping is scarier than losing money. This is key to Laoxiangji's steady development. |
| **Supply Chain is Root** thinking | A restaurant's root isn't in marketing or stores, it's in supply chain. Strong supply chain means strong business. Laoxiangji building a central kitchen in 2016 is exactly this reason. |
| **Long-termism/Mara-thon** thinking | Business development is a marathon, not a sprint. But don't go too slow when you need to move fast. Laoxiangji has gone from 1 store to 2000 stores in more than 20 years. |
| **Focus/Don't Chase Hot Trends** thinking | Don't be a pig in a hot trend, just be a tree you know well. But you can try if it connects with your roots. This is how Laoxiangji does it, focusing on Chinese-style fast food, but also trying prepared food as a supplement. |
| **Grassroots Practicality** thinking | Stay true to peasant roots, don't speak empty words, do practical things, stay grounded. This is the foundation of Laoxiangji's journey from Feixi, Anhui to nationwide. |
